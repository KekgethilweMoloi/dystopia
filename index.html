Colours of Dystopia

An imagined world or society in which people lead wretched, fearful lives; typically one that is totalitarian or post-apocalyptic.

Artistic renderings of dystopian societies show many different feelings and colours, knowing which one you are in determines what you need to survive.

Blue - Sparkling blue waters hide secrets of corruption and watery graves.

Green - Vibrant green surroundings, dotted with tall once completely occupied buildings; provide the perfect cover for beasts and monsters that see you before you see them.

Tan - Hot deserts, with high grey buildings acting as lightening rods for the dry and unforgiving wasteland.

Greyscale - An eerily quiet place, only the tapping of the keyboard can be heard. The souls of corporate slaves dying.
